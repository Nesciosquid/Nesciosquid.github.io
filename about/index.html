About

Aaron is a Computer Engineering PhD student at Boston University, where he builds tools for laboratory automation and cyber-physical systems.

He grew up in Menlo Park, CA and received a BS in Microbiology and a BA in Classical Civilizations from the University of California, Davis in 2012.
